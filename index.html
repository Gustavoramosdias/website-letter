Letter for mother

Letter to my mother,

I learned what love was from the first time I saw you. Your lap is my favorite place, nothing in this world compares to the feeling of having your affection, attention, care, something that only a mother can give, you know?

Every mother is irreplaceable. We can meet people along the way who help us walk. But a mother is a mother. In my heart, your space will always be unique. I know we've fought a lot, that we've disagreed at various times, but nothing can diminish love, the basis of everything.

Thank you for all the times you had to be tough on me. It made me grow! And the more I grow, the more I understand the reason for everything you did for me. I love you beyond measure, mom!

love you,
Gustavo Ramos Dias
